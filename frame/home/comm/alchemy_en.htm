＜About Alchemy＞
Alchemy is the conversion of excess resources by "alchemy equipment" into other resources.
・Alchemy is possible to three kinds at the same time. Two types are optional, and only rubbish will be a separate frame.
・The second is that resources in alchemy can not be alchemy.
・After conversion, you lose 10% of the original material before conversion. However, rubbish and high-speed building agents do not fall under this category.
・It takes 10 minutes to convert one set. If you set up conversion, the resources required will be consumed (delivered to the alchemy system).
　Although cancellation can not be performed on the way, when batch conversion is performed, except for one set being converted, it returns immediately.
・Funds, rubbish, high-speed build agents, you can choose the converted resources.
　Ammunition, nuclear stones and pyroxenes are dispersively transformed into two types of resources. (See the table below)
